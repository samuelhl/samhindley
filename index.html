Hi! My name is Samuel Hindley López and you've reached my personal website and online CV.

I was born and raised in Bracknell (England) but moved to the south of Spain when I was 8 years old. This gave me the advantage of learning English as I grew up and being forced to learn Spanish when I moved to Málaga. From the minute I touched the families first PC I was hooked on the world that is technology, from building and cleaning PC's to editing HTML code in a few blogs I created. From then on I steered my education and goals to where I am now, enrolled in a Master's degree in Computer Engineering.

Apart from studying I've been involved in a couple of projects that mainly involve web development as this is my favourite field (you can check them out in the 'Projects' section).

If you need any more information or a more extended version of my CV, please don't hesitate to contact me.
